Wireless Speaker Delay Measurement

Usage Instructions:

This tool plays a high-frequency sound to measure the delay of your wireless speaker. Please set your computer's sound output to the wireless speaker.

When prompted, grant microphone access. The tool will record a 4-second audio clip of the sound received through the microphone to calculate the delay. If the delay exceeds 4 seconds, the measurement may fail.

For best results, place your computer as close as possible to the speaker and keep the surrounding environment quiet.

If the measurement fails, you can manually set the delay in the extension popup.
